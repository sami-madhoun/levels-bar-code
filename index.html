Welcome to Levels Game Bar!

A place to reach new levels.

Levels Game Bar could be anything you want it to be. It is a bar, an arcade, a hangout spot, and much more. At Levels we focus on giving everyone the oppurtunity to play games and make some friends. We don't want anyone to feel left out so bring all your friends and prepare to have a full out brawl playing together!

We have different options for different people. We have a lounge room where you can choose between Playstation, Xbox or Nintendo Switch to play with friends, new friends or even alone. We also have a classic arcade room where u can find over 40 arcade machines to choose from. And last but certainly not least, we are a bar afterall so of course we have drinks! Enjoy a beer or even a glass of wine while playing your favourite games!
